MY FAVORITE PLACE (TINAPA RESORT)

My favorite place is Tinapa resort. It is a business and leisure resort located North of Calabar municipality, cross river,Nigeria. It is associated with the calabar free trade zone. It is a very beautiful site with a lot of fun stuff to enjoy all around

THINGS TO ENJOY AT TINAPA RESORT

  • The walkway

    • The Canopy walkway is a 100m swinging bridge suspended above the forest. To get there you can either drive famously winding 11km road from the bottom of the ill or take the cable car. Then afterwards you climb the walkway.

  • The resturant

    • The in-house resturant provides tasty three-course a-la-carte dinner with local wine or beer. They also have a lot of local delicacies but i absolutely love the double decker burger.

  • The swimming pool

    • In the pool you can have a lovely time cooling off when the weather is hot and blazing. It is an 8feet deep pool and can rarely accomodate kids.

  • The luxury beauty spa

    • In the luxury beauty spa, you ll find the balance and harmony of body and mind in a unique environment of high service and quality.
